I love Nursing

As a nurse, you are given the privilege and delicate responsibility of caring for the injured, sick and dying. This gives you the opportunity to make a difference in the lives of others by giving care to others in their time of need, just as you would want someone to do for you if you were in their place.

Nurses save lives by keeping a constant eye on their patients. With their years of health education, nurses know how to assess patients. When nurses notice a deterioration in their health, they make a rescue plan. In between all this surveillance and intervention, nurses educate their patients.
